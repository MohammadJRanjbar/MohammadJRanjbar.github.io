PersianMedQA

Language-Centric Evaluation of LLMs in the Persian Medical Domain

PersianMedQA is a large-scale, expert-validated multiple-choice question answering dataset covering 23 medical specialties, collected from 14 years of Iranian residency and pre-residency board examinations.

This dataset enables comprehensive evaluation of large language models in medical reasoning tasks, with questions available in both Persian (original) and high-quality English translations.

Dataset Features

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Questions span 23 medical specialties from cardiology to radiology
  • Clinical Focus: ~70% clinical case scenarios, 30% basic science questions
  • Rich Metadata: Specialty labels, clinical flags, patient demographics when available
  • Bilingual: Original Persian with high-quality English translations
  • Expert Validation: All questions verified by medical professionals

Intended Uses

  • Benchmarking multilingual and domain-specific language models on medical reasoning
  • Few-shot and zero-shot evaluation of Persian QA capabilities
  • Research on translation effects and cross-lingual performance
  • Cultural context analysis in medical AI systems
